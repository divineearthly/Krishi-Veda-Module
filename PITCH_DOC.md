This entire architecture—from C++ kernels to the FastAPI orchestration layer—was developed on a mobile phone. This serves as a 'Global Proof of Concept' for extreme resource efficiency and digital inclusion, proving that high-performance engineering can be harnessed by anyone, anywhere.

The platform enforces an organic-first safety gate. By calculating precise nutrient deficits via the Nikhilam kernel, it prevents fertilizer leaching and promotes 'Prakritik Kheti' (Natural Farming), directly supporting national soil health mandates.

**6. Current Status**:
Functional prototype complete. C++ shared libraries are compiled, Python-Vedic bridge is operational, and the FastAPI backend is ready for immediate mobile app integration.

**7. Goal of the Grant**:
